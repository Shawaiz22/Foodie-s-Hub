White Sauce Cheese Pasta

White Sauce cheezy pasta with mozerella cheese with some veggies.
Sprinkled with oregano and mixed herbs.

Ingridients Required:-

  • Pasta
  • Mayonnaise
  • Cream Cheese
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Milk
  • Garlic
  • Capsicum (or any other veggies if you like you can add)
  • Oregano
  • Butter
  • All purpose flour (Maida)
  • Salt to taste
  • Chilli flakes

Recipe:-

First of all Boil pasta around 70% and put it aside. Then take some butter in a pan and heat it at low flame. Then add the garlic and all the veggies you want. After that add the same amount of all purpose flour (maida) as butter into it and stir it until it gets mixed well (do it on a low flame otherwise it may burn the flour). After that add some milk to it and and then continiously stir until it becomes thick sauce. You may increase the quantity of milk as required if the sauce become too much thick, you can add more milk to it. After that add some mayyonaise and cream cheese into it to make it more cheesy and creamy. After this, add the boiled pasta we had put aside, into it. After doing this, add salt to taste and then add some amount to mozarella cheese into it. Now its texture will become so creamy and cheesy you will feel the spread of cheese while stiring it. Now add the oregano seasoning and also chilli flakes to it. Finally it is ready to be served.
Garnish it with some coriander leaves and sprinkle some mixed herb seasoning, chilli flakes and cheese to it.
The amazing white sauce cheese pasta is ready to eat!!!
